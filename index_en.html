About us

Here in FINSYS we specialize in custom software development for financial institutions. We are an experienced team of application specialists in IT business IT. Finsys efficiency stands out. We use modern and proven technologies in development and project management, so that we can provide services at the highest level of quality. Thanks to good cooperation with our business customers, our work brings tangible benefits to their business.

Architecture design

If needed, we can design an efficient, scalable architecture for you. We can also integrate your new solution into the existing one. All we need is your idea.

Development

We will develop the solution in the technology of your choice or will help you choose the most appropriate one. We use the most tested ones, matching and customizing them to the needs of your project.

Quality

The quality of code is our hobby. We are obsessively sensitive to the use of good programming habits and clean code. Before merging the changes to the production branch, the code is reviewed by at least one experienced architect.

Transparency

We value transparency - we work in Scrum. In day-to-day work we use issue management systems such as Jira. GIT is our version control system.

Friendly support

We are always available for you - you will never be left alone with potential problems or doubts.

Deployment help

Depending on your needs, we will deploy the solution on your own infrastructure or in the Cloud.

The team

If you give a good idea to a mediocre group, they'll screw it up. If you give a mediocre idea to a good group, they'll fix it. Or they'll throw it away and come up with something else.

Jeff Atwood

Finsys is a team of consultants (architects, designers and developers) operating under one brand for many years.
The core team and founders of Finsys are three people with vast experience in developing complete IT solutions, primarily in banking and insurance area. We work closely with people with similar profiles and professional experience, which makes our team competitive on the IT services market. Depending on the demand, Finsys can provide more skilled developers to work on your project. Our team members have taken a significant part in the deployment, integration and development of large IT solutions such as:
  • SWIFTAlliance - build an advanced integration platform for all SWIFT systems.
  • Calypso - Development and customization for customer needs, integration with other FrontOffice systems and reporting systems
  • Kondor+ - customization of Reuters' system, integration with other systems
  • FlexCube - integration with other bank systems, support for implementation of payments
  • Build our own integration platform for multiple systems at the service level
  • Prototype implementation of a system for cash deposits handling (POS technology), for the Bank in Turkmenistan
  • Profile-Sanchez - integration with all systems in the bank, based on the in-house developed JDBC driver
  • TBMS-Suntech - integration with the centralized billing and commissioning system
  • Murex - BackOffice systems integration
  • Architectural design and development of a nationwide scoped web application for medical clinics.
